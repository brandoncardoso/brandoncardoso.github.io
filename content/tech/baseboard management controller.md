---
title: Baseboard Management Controller
tags: []
---

* a specialized microcontroller embedded in the motherboard of a device (server, switch, etc.)
  * usually ARM based with onboard graphics, logic, DRAM
* allows remote monitoring and management of device without requiring a physical connection
  * power management
  * fan speeds
  * component health/metrics
  * mount ISOs
  * system event logs (SEL)
* if motherboard has standby power, you can still access the BMC while the host device is offline
