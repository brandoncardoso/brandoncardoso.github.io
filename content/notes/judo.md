## Techniques

* **投技（なげわざ）**- throwing
    * 手技
    * 腰技
    * 足技
    * 真捨身技（ますてみわざ）- full sacrifice
    * 横捨身技（よこすてみわざ）- side sacrifice
* **固技（かためわざ）**- grappling
    * 抑込技（おさえこみわざ）- holds
    * 絞技（しめわざ）- chokes
    * 関節技（かんせつわざ）- joint locks

## Scoring

* **一本（いっぽん）**_lit. "1 full point"_
    * throwing your opponent on their back, applying a technique or countering the opponent's technique, with considerable ability and efficiency
    * **勢い（いきおい）**- momentum with force and speed
    * **弾み（はずみ）**- skillful impulse/stimulus to momentum, sharpness, rhythm
* **技あり（わざあり）**_lit. "with skill"_
    * given if some criteria of　一本 is not fully achieved
    * receiving 2 技あり is equivalent to 一本 (技あり合わせて一本)

In a match, victory is achieved after receiving an 一本.
