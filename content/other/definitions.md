title: Definitions
---

* **bathetic** _(adj.)_ -- characterized by or pertaining to bathos
* **bathos** _(noun)_
    1. an abrupt, unintended juxtaposition between the exalted and the commonplace, producing a ludicrous effect
    2. an anticlimax
    3. insincere or grossly sentimental pathos
* **flebile** *(adj.)* -- feeble, faint, sad/sorrowful tone
* **pedagogue** *(noun)*
    1. a teacher or educator
    2. someone that is dull, formal, pedantic
* **steelman** _(noun)_ -- a strong or effective opposition to an argument
* **tegular** _(adj.)_ -- relating to or resembling a tile, consisting of tiles, arranged like tiles
